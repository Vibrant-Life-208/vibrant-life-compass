Vibrant Life · A formation for ages 15 to 18

The Launch Pad Story

Origin · The Forge · The Helm - a formation, not a high school

We help young people become who they are meant to be - to know themselves, do work that matters, make the hard calls, and love the life they are living.

How to read the wheel

The program turns like seasons. Every learner walks all three seasons before they launch, and may join at any season - a learner who starts mid-cycle is joining the story in progress, never behind. Each learner carries one Captain's Log the whole way; it is the line they walk through the turning circle.

Your Next Great Adventure A personal graduation send-off in each learner's final year, whatever season that is. The send-off belongs to the learner, not to the calendar.

How it works

The shape

A turning cycle, not a ladder. Three seasons; walk all three before you launch. Join at any season, never behind.

The week

Two days with a Launch Pad Guide - a life-coach, not a teacher - plus two days of self-directed project work and a protected day for rest, play, and friendship.

The thread

One learner-owned Captain's Log, carried and deepened across all three seasons. It belongs to the learner.

The send-off

Your Next Great Adventure - a personal graduation rite in each learner's final year.

The floor

Learner-led. No grades, no ranking. Joy is woven into the work, never assigned.
