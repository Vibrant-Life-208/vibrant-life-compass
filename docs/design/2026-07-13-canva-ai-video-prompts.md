# Canva AI Video Prompts - Vibrant Life Playbook Videos

**For:** testing Canva's AI video generation (Magic Media text-to-video) for the four playbook videos.
**Style locked:** **cartoon / animated** - never photorealistic children. This is the Level-6 gate: an illustrated style can't misrepresent a real studio or ask a parent to feel something about kids who don't exist. Warm, not slick.
**Companion scripts (the source of truth for content):** `2026-06-30-family-playbook-video-scripts-v0.2-additive.md`

---

## Read this first (how to use these)

- **Paste the STYLE ANCHOR at the start of every prompt.** Canva's text-to-video makes short clips (a few seconds each); the anchor is what keeps them looking like one film. Consistency across clips is the known weak spot - the repeated style words are your best defense.
- **Generate scene-by-scene, then assemble on the Canva timeline** (drag clips in order, add captions, add the voiceover). Don't expect one prompt to make the whole video.
- **No words in the video.** Ask for no text/letters/logos in the generation - Canva adds captions and the VL logo separately, cleaner.
- **Voice:** record **Jenna's real voice** over the finished cartoon (phone audio is fine). Cartoon visuals + her real warmth = the whole thing. Tool TTS only as a rough-cut placeholder.
- **The bar (from the room):** "she pictures her child here," not "what a well-produced video." If a clip feels slick or uncanny, regenerate warmer/simpler.

### STYLE ANCHOR (paste at the start of every prompt)
> *Warm hand-drawn 2D cartoon, flat children's-picture-book illustration with soft grain, rounded friendly shapes, gentle earthy palette of spring green, warm terracotta orange, soft cream and sky blue, soft natural light, cozy and hopeful mood, simple diverse characters with rounded features, gentle motion.*

### NEGATIVE / AVOID (add to every prompt if Canva has a negative field, or append)
> *no text, no words, no letters, no captions, no logos, no brand marks, no photorealism, no real photographs, no uncanny or creepy faces, not glossy, not corporate.*

### The reusable cast (describe them the SAME way every time)
- **Guide:** a warm adult guide, soft cardigan, kind eyes, hair in a loose bun, calm posture.
- **Learners:** young children, simple rounded features, diverse skin tones, casual play clothes and rain boots.
- **Setting:** a bright, plant-filled learning studio and a grassy field with a big tree; muddy boots and real weather.
