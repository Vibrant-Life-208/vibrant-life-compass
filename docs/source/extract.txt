                                                  attendance policy
                                                                          With VL being part-time we really power pack the time when we are in studio. Please try to plan
                                                                          family trips on long weekends or break weeks. If you miss days, it’s life—we get it. We don’t keep
                                                                          attendance. When a learner is out it’s their responsibility to check in with their studio and make a
                                                                          plan, especially for any Quest roles that depend on them. Core is flexible and can often be done at
                                                                          home. As Journey Tracker rolls out, more work will be accessible there.

                                                                          Absences (Expected and Unexpected):
                                                                          Send a BAND message to your guide to let them know. Feel free to CC Jenna.
                                          6                                                                                                  7                                                                                 8                                                                               9                                                                           10
                                                                          Late arrival:
                                                                          No need to message, just come when you can.
                                                                             Younger learners: please park in rocks or dirt lot and walk them to the studio door.
                                                                             Older learners: you may drop off at the roundabout; wait to visually confirm they’ve entered their
                                                                             studio (their tribe may be outside or with another studio).

                                                                          Early pick up:
                                                                           Best practice: tell your learner in advance so they know to be ready. Messaging the guide is optional,
                                                                          ultimately the learner is responsible to be prepared.
                                                                              For younger learners, you can pop into the studio to let them know it’s time.
                                                                              Please park on the side rocks or in the dirt lot (not the roundabout).
                                                                              Each studio has its own check-out system; ask your learner to confirm they completed it before
                                                                              leaving.


                                       11                                                                                                12

                                                                          any closures. Our basic philosophy is, we will be in session, and it’s up to each family to decide what

                                                                          your family. This allows for each family's comfort level, and even car performance, to factor into their
                                                                          individual decisions. Often we have combined play time during snowy days- it’s a blast.
