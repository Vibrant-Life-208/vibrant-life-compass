                                                   INTRODUCTION + PURPOSE




                                                     WHAT WE STAND FOR

                            1. Learners are whole people.
                            They arrive with creativity, emotions, curiosity, and voice. They are
                            geniuses. We honor both their hearts and minds — and make space for
                            growth in both.

                            2. Families are partners & the primary educators.
                            We believe in walking besides families. Parents have the primary
                            responsibility for their child’s education. We are here to support that.
                            When the family and VL are united in the shared vision the learner can
                            thrive.

                            3. The learning environment matters.
                            We design inspiring spaces where learners can feel safe, challenged, and
                            have a voice in the program. It’s not about being perfect, it’s about being
                            intentional, seeing challenges, embracing them and evolving.

                            4. Purpose leads the way.
                            We are not here to simply “do school.” We are here to help learners
                            discover who they are and what they’re called to bring to the world.



                            How to Use This Book
                              Read it. Use it. Revisit it.
                              If you ask a question we’ve answered here, we’ll lovingly guide you
                              back to it. Not because we don’t care — but because we do.
                              This is the learner led way: trusting people with the responsibility to
                              own their journey as much as possible.
