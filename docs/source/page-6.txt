                                                   INTRODUCTION + PURPOSE




                                  WELCOME TO THE PLAYBOOK
                               A guide to how we live and grow together at
                               Vibrant Life. This playbook isn’t a rulebook. It’s a
                               guide — a shared understanding of how we
                               move through this journey together as a
                               community of families, learners, and guides. It
                               will continue to evolve as we change.



                            WE CREATED THIS FOR THREE MAIN
                                      REASONS:

                        1. To help you feel grounded. When questions arise (and they will!),
                                   this is your place to find answers and reconnect.

                       2. To build clarity and trust. Clear agreements, and understanding
                            help us stay focused on growth, not confusion or conflict.

                         3. To honor the heart of what we’re building. This isn’t just about
                        logistics. It’s about creating a space that supports the learner and
                                               the family to new heights.
