                       At Vibrant Life, we believe food plays an important role in overall health—and that healthy
                       eating habits are a life skill. We encourage families to send nourishing foods while respecting
                       different dietary philosophies. When doable, please limit high-sugar items (sports/energy
                       drinks, soda, candy, and dessert-style snacks).

                       Water: Please send a labeled water bottle each day (a spare can stay in the cubby). If a learner
                       forgets they may borrow a studio cup and are responsible for washing it that day. The studio is
                       closed for refills during outside play, so you can remind your learner to fill up before heading
                       out.

                     celebrations
                          We love celebrations and traditions at Vibrant Life, and birthdays are no different! Each
                          studio has its own birthday traditions, and all include a “Circle of Appreciation.” The
                          birthday child sits in the center while peers offer one sincere, character-based
                          compliment; the child closes by sharing one thing they appreciate about themselves.
                          Each learner also receives a peacock feather as a reminder of their unique beauty.




                                                                        Birthday Treats: Learners are invited (when
                                                                        possible) to bake a snack or bring a health-
                                                                        conscious treat to share at lunch. Parents are
                                                                        welcome to join—please coordinate with your
                                                                        guide via a BAND message.
