                     THE "LEARNER LED WAY" IS BEST THOUGHT OF AS A GAME

                                     This is our favorite analogy to help illustrate why the rules of this game
                                    must be clear to all of the families and learners who join the community.




                                                           Imagine you are playing a game of
                                                           soccer…
                                                   ...and someone comes in and starts to
                                                   use their hands to play.




                              Are you still playing soccer at that point?


                              Perhaps it's handball or just a fun game,
                              but if people continue to play with their
                              hands, it is no longer soccer.
