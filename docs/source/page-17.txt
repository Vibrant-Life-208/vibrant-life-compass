                               cheerleader, game maker, asker of

                          As a Guide, I promise to:
                           1. See you as a genius with a gift to offer the world
                           2. Uphold the integrity of a learner led environment, turning over responsibilities to you, the learners,
                              as quickly as possible
                           3. Uphold the studio guardrails ensuring the foundational physical and emotional safety for our tribe
                           4. Rather than giving answers to something you can discover on your own (and robing you of that
                              accomplishment & development), I will use Socratic responses, growth minded encouragement,
                              and see what deeper lessons can be addressed. I will continuously work to equip the studio so you
                              have the tools available to find your answer. Questions are opportunities for deeper thinking and
                              development, they are not taken lightly and are not something to be ignored.
                           5. Provide launches, quests, challenges, processes, frameworks, examples, recipes, to inspire, equip
                              and connect.
                           6. Allow you to learn from failure & own your studio and natural consequences as necessary.
                           7. Be on my own Hero’s Journey, evolving right alongside you
                           8. Celebrate your effort, courage and progress. Offer growth mindset support when the path is
                              difficult.
                           9. Be prepared and on time every day to the best of my ability
                          10. Ask for feedback, receive 360 reviews to continue to evolve and improve as a guide
