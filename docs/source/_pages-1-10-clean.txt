                                  WELCOME TO THE PLAYBOOK
                               A guide to how we live and grow together at
                               Vibrant Life. This playbook isn’t a rulebook. It’s a
                               guide — a shared understanding of how we
                               move through this journey together as a
                               community of families, learners, and guides. It
                               will continue to evolve as we change.



                            WE CREATED THIS FOR THREE MAIN
                                      REASONS:

                        1. To help you feel grounded. When questions arise (and they will!),
                                   this is your place to find answers and reconnect.

                       2. To build clarity and trust. Clear agreements, and understanding
                            help us stay focused on growth, not confusion or conflict.

                         3. To honor the heart of what we’re building. This isn’t just about
                        logistics. It’s about creating a space that supports the learner and
                                               the family to new heights.

                                                   INTRODUCTION + PURPOSE




                                                     WHAT WE STAND FOR

                            1. Learners are whole people.
                            They arrive with creativity, emotions, curiosity, and voice. They are
                            geniuses. We honor both their hearts and minds — and make space for
                            growth in both.

                            2. Families are partners & the primary educators.
                            We believe in walking besides families. Parents have the primary
                            responsibility for their child’s education. We are here to support that.
                            When the family and VL are united in the shared vision the learner can
                            thrive.

                            3. The learning environment matters.
                            We design inspiring spaces where learners can feel safe, challenged, and
                            have a voice in the program. It’s not about being perfect, it’s about being
                            intentional, seeing challenges, embracing them and evolving.

                            4. Purpose leads the way.
                            We are not here to simply “do school.” We are here to help learners
                            discover who they are and what they’re called to bring to the world.



                            How to Use This Book
                              Read it. Use it. Revisit it.
                              If you ask a question we’ve answered here, we’ll lovingly guide you
                              back to it. Not because we don’t care — but because we do.
                              This is the learner led way: trusting people with the responsibility to
                              own their journey as much as possible.

                                          WHY THE RULES NEED TO BE SO CLEAR




                     THE "LEARNER LED WAY" IS BEST THOUGHT OF AS A GAME

                                     This is our favorite analogy to help illustrate why the rules of this game
                                    must be clear to all of the families and learners who join the community.




                                                           Imagine you are playing a game of
                                                           soccer…
                                                   ...and someone comes in and starts to
                                                   use their hands to play.




                              Are you still playing soccer at that point?


                              Perhaps it's handball or just a fun game,
                              but if people continue to play with their
                              hands, it is no longer soccer.

                      VIBRANT LIFE IS DIFFERENT BY DESIGN
                        It looks and feels unlike any other school experience. By clearly outlining how we
                        play together, educate on key mindsets, and creating agreements with one
                        another, we can best serve your family and deliver on our promises to you.
