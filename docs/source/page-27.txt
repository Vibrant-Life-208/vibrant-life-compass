                         Thresholds for Thriving: In Acton studios across the world, learners advance by
                         readiness, not by birthdays. When clear thresholds (habits of self-governance,
                         completed goals, and demonstrated mastery) are met—and the learner has
                         genuinely grown—new privileges and the next studio open up. This models the real
                         world: accomplishments unlock opportunity.

                         If your child is age-eligible, guides will share the specific thresholds they’ll work
                         toward and, by May, your learner will prepare a “Pitch to the Next Studio” showing
                         evidence of growth (badges, and reflections). If thresholds aren’t yet met, no
                         problem—the path continues until readiness occurs! We will educate parents more
                         on this process during our last Parent Meeting.
