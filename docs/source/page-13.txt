                      At Vibrant Life we promise that learners will find a calling that will change the
                      world by Learning to Learn, Learning to Do, and Learning to Be.


                                                                    Learning to LEARN means finding the recipes,
                        LEARNING TO LEARN                           processes, tools, and algorithms that help us make
                                                                    better decisions and more deeply embed the right
                                    (Processes)                     habits of critical thinking.



                                                                    Learning to DO means making practical decisions
                           LEARNING TO DO                           and actions in the real world to accomplish great
                      (Skills + Academics + Real World)             tasks, as a way to change the world.




                        However important the tasks of learning to learn and learning to do, the most important
                        part of finding the Grail in the Hero’s Journey is not the Grail itself, but how the hero is
                        changed in the process. This is the essence of Learning to BE.


                              "We delight in the beauty of the butterfly but rarely admit the changes
                              it has gone through to achieve that beauty." - Maya Angelou

                                                                    We want these people near the end of a
                                                                    successful, satisfying, and fulfilling life to have
                                                                    answers to three important questions:
                                                                        Did I contribute something meaningful?
                                                                        Did I actively create the life I wanted?

                            LEARNING TO BE                              Did I give and receive love to the best of my ability?


                     (Character + Matters of the Heart) The final “learning to be” promise is to help each
                                                                    learner meet hard moments with courage, build
                                                                    durable habits of positive character, and
                                                                    transform energy-draining habits into practices
                                                                    that support the highest version of themselves.
