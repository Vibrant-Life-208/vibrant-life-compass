                              PARENTS                        LEARNERS                                  GUIDES




                     role of each player:
                     Creating clear, consistent expectations for Vibrant Life's families is an important part
                     of our program. As such, we have outlined the roles of the parents, learners, and staff
                     so that all players understand their role and can clearly see the role of other players
                     as well. Each player must agree to do their best to uphold their role.

                     The purpose of this is not to promote rule-following or bureaucracy but rather the
                     opposite: to create clarity to encourage innovation between learners, parents, and VL
                     staff.
